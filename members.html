Faculty Members

I received my B.S. and M.S. from Fudan University, Shanghai, P. R. China, in 1992 and 1995 respectively and the Ph.D. from University of Iowa, Iowa City, in 1999. Here are my latest curriculum vitae and short biography. See the latest news for my group.


I am the director of Mixed-Signal Nanometer VLSI Research Lab at UC Riverside. I am also a cooperating faculty member (co-faculty) of the Computer Science and Engineering department, and the Associate Director of the Computer Engineer (CEN) program at Bourns College of Engineering.
